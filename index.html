About


I am currently a Research Associate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Oxford from August 2021. I work in the Cyber-Physical Systems Group under the supervision of Prof. Niki Trigoni and Andrew Markham. Prior to Oxford, I obtained his PhD degree in 2021 from China and was supervised by Prof. Jun Wang. In 2019, I went to Cardiff University, UK, as a joint PhD for 18 months in the School of Computer Science and Informatics, under the supervision of Prof. Yu-kun Lai and Jing Wu. My research interests are 3D Vision, Point Cloud Processing, and Scene Understanding, and publishes extensively in venues and journals such as CVPR, ICCV, RA-L, TMM, IJCV, and etc.
